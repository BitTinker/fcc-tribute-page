C.S. Lewis-The Professor

  1. Lewis' early life
    • Born 29 November 1898
    • Grew up in Belfast and attended school at Watford and Malvern
    • Privately tutored by W.T. Kirkpatrick
  2. Lewis' army service
    • Entered British Army as second lieutenant after completing officer training in 1917
    • Experienced trench warfare during Battle of the Somme
    • Wounded by friendly shellfire and discharged
  3. Lewis joins the college
    • Reenters Oxford College, where he had begun his studies before joining the Army
    • Received Firsts in Honour Moderations, Greats, and English in 1920-1923
  4. Lewis' academic career
    • Lewis studes Medieval literature
    • Writes for Oxford History of English Literature
    • Lewis lectures exensively at Magdalen College
